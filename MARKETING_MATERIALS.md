Create interactive calculator at `/tools/roi-calculator`:

**Inputs:**

- Current monthly shipments
- Average shipment distance
- Number of drivers
- Current fuel efficiency (MPG)
- Average shipment value

**Calculated Savings:**

- Fuel savings: `(distance * shipments * (targetMPG - currentMPG) / currentMPG) * fuelPrice * 12`
- Time savings: `(shipments * 0.4 * avgTimePerShipment) * hourlyRate * 12`
- Support reduction: `(supportCalls * 0.6 * costPerCall) * 12`
- **Total Annual ROI**
